About the Company

IBM (International Business Machines Corporation) is a multinational technology company that has been at the forefront of innovation and technology solutions for over a century. Founded in 1911, IBM has evolved from a manufacturer of punch card tabulating machines to a leading provider of advanced technologies and consulting services.

IBM serves clients across various sectors, including finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, government, and more. Its client base includes small businesses, midsize enterprises, and large multinational corporations.

IBM is committed to research and development, investing heavily in emerging technologies and driving innovation. The company has a long-standing reputation for its scientific research, and it holds a significant number of patents in various technology domains.

IBM also values corporate social responsibility and sustainability, striving to make a positive impact through initiatives focused on environmental conservation, education, and community development.

Overall, IBM is recognized for its vast technological expertise, extensive product and service offerings, and its commitment to helping organizations leverage advanced technologies to solve complex problems and drive digital transformation.
