About the Company

HCL Technologies is a global information technology (IT) services and consulting company headquartered in Noida, India. Established in 1976, HCL has grown into one of the largest IT services firms in India and has a presence in over 50 countries. The company offers a wide range of services and solutions to clients across various industries.

HCL serves clients across industries, including banking and financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, telecommunications, and more. The company has a global customer base, ranging from small and medium-sized enterprises to large multinational corporations.
